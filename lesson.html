Lessons

Brittany has been teaching privately since beginning college, where she taught violin and viola lessons in the String Project at Arizona State University. Her passion for teaching grew as she learned more about the violin herself throughout school. Her methods of teaching are individualized for each student, depending on their level and their goals. Right now Brittany maintains a private violin/viola studio throughout the valley. She is based in Mesa and traveling to students in the Gilbert, Chandler, Tempe and Scottsdale areas. She is currently accepting students of all ages and levels. Contact below for availability and pricing!
