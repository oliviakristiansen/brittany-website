Biography

Brittany is a classically trained violinist from Mesa, AZ. She graduated from Mesa High School and also performed in the West Region and Arizona All-State Orchestras. She completed her Bachelor's degree summa cum laude and her Master's degree both in violin performance from Arizona State University under Dr. Jonathan Swartz. While at ASU, Brittany performed in the ASU Symphony Orchestra, Lyric Opera Theatre Orchestra and in many student composition recitals. She received training in the Visiting Quartet Residency Program under the Brentano, St. Lawrence, Juilliard and Shanghai String Quartets. She also had the opportunity to receive instruction from world renown violinists Vadim Gluzman, Mark Peskanov and Rachel Barton Pine. Her summers have taken her to study at Le Domaine Forget Summer Music Academy in Quebec, Canada and Madeline Island Music Camp in Madeline Island, Wisconsin.
